# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance data to uncover trends, evaluate product performance, and generate actionable business insights. The dashboard was developed using **Microsoft Excel**, with data cleaning and transformation carried out in **Microsoft Excel** and **Power Query**.

The goal is to provide a clear, interactive view of sales performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor revenue and customer activity.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing products.
- Track order trends over time.
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
- Provide interactive filtering (slicers) for product and Orderstatus analysis.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Pivot Tabe

The dataset was cleaned and transformed prior to analysis to ensure accuracy and consistency.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue Performance
- The business generated a total revenue of **$1.26M**.
- Average revenue per customer/transaction is **$1,053.97**, indicating strong transaction value.

### 📦 Product Performance
- Chairs and Printers recorded the highest sales volumes, indicating strong customer demand in these categories.
- Phones recorded the lowest sales volume, suggesting potential challenges in demand or positioning.

### 📈 Order Trends
- A general decline in orders was observed across the years analyzed.
- Monthly sales peaked around June before declining in the latter half of the year.

### 🚚 Order Status Analysis
- Cancelled and returned orders accounted for a significant proportion of total orders, indicating areas for operational improvement.

### 📢  Referral source Analysis 
- Instragram and Email was the highest source of referral.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Investigate causes of high cancellation and return rates.
- Focus marketing strategies on high-performing product categories.
- Introduce promotional campaigns during low-sales periods.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies using referral insights.
